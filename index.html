PROCESS

DESIGN

Create visual solutions that align with the client's objectives. The process often involves collaboration, feedback, and iterations to ensure the final design meets the client's expectations.

DETAIL

Detail drawing stage is a critical part of the design process, translating conceptual ideas into practical, actionable instructions for construction or manufacturing teams. It serves as a bridge between the creative design phase and the actual realization of the project.

BUDGET

Budget for a design project involves carefully estimating and allocating resources to ensure that the project is completed successfully within financial constraints. Creating an accurate budget is an iterative process, and adjustments may be necessary as the project progresses

BUILD

Processes involve collaboration with stakeholders, regulatory compliance, quality control, and project closeout to ensure the successful realization of the design vision. Effective communication and coordination are essential throughout the entire process.

"Architecture is the thoughtful making of space"

A deliberate and conscious process of creating spaces that are not only functional but also aesthetically pleasing and culturally relevant.
