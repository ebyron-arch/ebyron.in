Detail drawing stage is a critical part of the design process, translating conceptual ideas into practical, actionable instructions for construction or manufacturing teams. It serves as a bridge between the creative design phase and the actual realization of the project.

BUDGET

Budget for a design project involves carefully estimating and allocating resources to ensure that the project is completed successfully within financial constraints. Creating an accurate budget is an iterative process, and adjustments may be necessary as the project progresses
